Hello! I am currently an undergraduate studying computer science at Cornell University. I am applying for computer science PhD programs (start Fall 2026), with an interest in machine learning and natural language processing.

I have served as a TA for Natural Language Processing (CS 4740) and Mathematical Foundations of Computing (CS 2800), as Data Science Lead for Cornell Data Science, and as Course Manager and Lecturer for INFO 1998, an introduction to machine learning.

In my free time, I am an avid reader, enjoy learning languages (I have an 850+ day streak on Duolingo), and love making caffeinated beverages of every variety.
