Selected recent reads & thoughts

  • Emma
    by Jane Austen
    ★ ★ ★ ★ ★

    I will admit to struggling with Emma as a character till the very end; her behavior towards Harriet would be unforgivable if it hadn't turned out okay in the end (with no credit to Emma). I did enjoy the complete and vivid depiction of Highbury society. In my eyes, P&P and S&S are more romantic and boast characters who are much easier to sympathize with, if less fleshed out.

  • The Well of Ascension
    by Brandon Sanderson
    ★ ★ ★ ★ ★

    As a continuation to Mistborn, it succeeds very well. I love the cast of characters, and can forgive the somewhat clumsy pacing and heavy handed character development (especially as it regards Vin, Zane, and Elend).

  • Four Thousand Weeks: Time Management for Mortals
    by Oliver Burkeman
    ★ ★ ★ ★ ★

    A reality check for overachievers. Insightful, but it didn't change my life. Maybe it'll change yours?
